The Story Behind.

This project is for the UW CSE Capstone course in virtual and augmented reality. Given the circumstances surrounding the coronavirus that hit the world in 2020, we wanted to create an experience where people could get together in VR, and relax together. We picked sailing because of our shared interest in the great outdoors and drew inspiration from the Puget Sound. Throughout the 10 weeks of Spring quarter, we each worked from home, staying connected through Slack and many many Zoom calls. You can read each week of our progress in the posts on this website, and learn more about the course on the course webpage!
